Villa Overview

This spacious villa in Hanioti, Chalkidiki, offers 4 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, making it an ideal retreat for families or groups seeking comfort and relaxation. Enjoy a private pool, modern amenities, and a prime location just steps away from the vibrant village center and beautiful beaches.

The villa features a fully equipped kitchen, a cozy living area with a flat-screen TV, and a private outdoor space with BBQ facilities. WiFi is available throughout the property, and parking is provided for your convenience.

Additional amenities include breakfast options and a play zone for children, ensuring a delightful stay for guests of all ages. Airport shuttle services and pet-friendly options are available upon request.
