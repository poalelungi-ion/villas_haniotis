Fun and Safe Play Zone for Kids

At Villas Hanioti, we understand that a family vacation should be enjoyable for everyone, including the little ones. Our dedicated Play Zone is designed to keep children entertained with safe, fun, and engaging activities, allowing parents to relax and unwind. Located within the villa premises, the Play Zone offers a secure environment where kids can explore and play freely.

  • Age-appropriate play equipment including slides, swings, and climbing structures.
  • Soft, cushioned flooring to ensure safety during playtime.
  • Shaded areas to protect kids from the sun while they enjoy outdoor fun.

The Play Zone is conveniently located near the villas, making it easy for parents to keep an eye on their children while enjoying the private pool or BBQ facilities. It’s the perfect spot for kids to make new friends, burn off energy, and create lasting vacation memories.

Whether your children love imaginative play or active adventures, our Play Zone caters to a variety of interests. This family-friendly feature ensures that Villas Hanioti is not just a luxurious retreat for adults, but a delightful haven for kids too.
