Experience Outdoor Dining with Our BBQ Facilities

At Villas Hanioti, we provide state-of-the-art BBQ facilities to enhance your vacation experience. Whether you're planning a family barbecue or a casual gathering with friends, our outdoor grilling areas are designed for convenience and enjoyment. Each villa is equipped with a modern BBQ setup, complete with all the essentials you need for a perfect meal under the sun or stars.

  • High-quality gas and charcoal grills available for versatile cooking options.
  • Spacious outdoor dining areas with seating for up to 10 guests.
  • Complimentary BBQ utensils, including tongs, spatulas, and cleaning tools.
  • Shaded pergolas and ambient lighting for comfortable day or night use.

Our BBQ facilities are located in beautifully landscaped gardens, offering a scenic backdrop for your meals. We ensure that all equipment is maintained to the highest standards, with regular cleaning and safety checks. Guests can also request optional extras, such as premium charcoal or pre-prepared ingredient kits, to make your barbecue experience even more seamless.

To use the BBQ facilities, simply reserve your preferred time slot through our villa management team during your stay. We recommend booking in advance during peak seasons to ensure availability. Whether you're grilling fresh seafood from the local market or savoring classic barbecue dishes, Villas Hanioti's BBQ facilities are designed to create unforgettable dining moments.
