    Who We Are

    Luxury Villas in Hanioti, Chalkidiki

    Situated in Hanioti, just 1.6 km from Hanioti Beach, Villas Hanioti offers luxurious accommodations with sea views, free WiFi, and private parking.

    At Villas Hanioti, we provide spacious 3 and 4-bedroom villas designed for ultimate comfort and relaxation. Each villa comes with a private pool offering stunning pool and garden views, perfect for a refreshing dip or a relaxing evening by the water. Enjoy complimentary high-speed WiFi throughout the property, ensuring you stay connected during your stay. We offer free private parking on-site for all guests, making it convenient for those traveling by car. For added flexibility, car rental services are available upon request to explore the beautiful Chalkidiki region. Our modern amenities include air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and fully equipped BBQ facilities for outdoor dining. Families will appreciate the picnic area, and pet-friendly options are available upon request, ensuring a welcoming stay for everyone. Additional services like airport shuttles can also be arranged to enhance your vacation experience.
