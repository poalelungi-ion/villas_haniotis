Villa Overview

This elegant villa in Hanioti, Chalkidiki, offers 3 bedrooms and 1 bathroom, making it a perfect retreat for families or small groups seeking comfort and relaxation. Enjoy a private 50 sq.m. pool, modern amenities, and a location just 900m from Haniotis Beach and the vibrant village center.

Spread over two floors, the villa features a fully equipped kitchen, a large living room with a sofa bed and flat-screen TV, and an outdoor area with a portable BBQ, garden swing, and deck chairs. WiFi is available throughout, and parking is included.

Additional services include daily cleaning, a welcome basket with wine and fruit, and a playground for children. Optional add-ons such as car rental, airport transfers, boat rentals, and guided excursions can be arranged upon request to enhance your stay.
