Who We Are

Luxury Living in Chalkidiki

Villas Hanioti is dedicated to providing exceptional vacation experiences with our luxurious villas in the heart of Hanioti, Chalkidiki, offering a wide range of services to ensure a memorable stay.

  • Spacious villas with private pools and modern amenities, including air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and soundproofing.
  • Free high-speed WiFi throughout the property to keep you connected.
  • Free private parking on-site for all guests, with car rental services available upon request.
  • BBQ facilities for outdoor dining, complemented by a picnic area for families.
  • Airport shuttle services available upon request for a seamless travel experience.
  • Pet-friendly options available upon request, ensuring a welcoming stay for everyone.
